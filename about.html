Heerak Food
15-Year Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in Ahmedabad, we’ve been dedicated to delivering quality food products for 15 years. Join us as we celebrate our legacy!

Heerak’s journey began in 2010, founded by Mr. Ketanbhai Thakkar and his parents, Mr. Krishnakant and Mrs. Manjulaben Thakkar, as a part-time venture selling mamra from a single room in their home. Within two years, they introduced two pack sizes of mamra and expanded to a rented space by 2013, offering three varieties with innovative packaging. Heerak’s unique three-step cleaning process set it apart, earning customer trust. By 2014, modern machines improved efficiency, and by 2017, the business scaled to three rented locations, adding poha and premium jaggery to its offerings. By 2020, they celebrated 10 years with over 20 products and a large factory. Despite a 2022 rebranding from Kohinoor to Heerak, the brand thrived, reaching 35+ products and supplying 500+ stores in Ahmedabad by 2024, with a 90% customer return rate. With a daily production capacity of 1 ton—10 times that of its early days—Heerak stands as a symbol of innovation and quality, aspiring to expand nationwide and continue serving loyal customers with premium products. Looking ahead, Heerak aspires to expand its footprint nationwide and continue innovating new products to serve its loyal customers better.
